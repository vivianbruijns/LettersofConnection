Our Story

The journey of Letters of Connection

How It All Began

Letters of Connection was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to combat social isolation among elderly community members while providing students with valuable life lessons and perspectives from older generations. We recognized that in our increasingly digital world, the personal touch of handwritten correspondence was being lost, along with opportunities for meaningful intergenerational connections.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we've facilitated thousands of letter exchanges between students and elderly participants. These connections have resulted in lasting friendships, reduced feelings of loneliness, and created a valuable exchange of wisdom, experience, and fresh perspectives.

Our Values

  • Connection: We believe in the power of human connection across generations.

  • Growth: Both students and elderly participants learn and grow through their correspondence.
