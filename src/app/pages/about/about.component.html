About Letters of Connection

Building bridges between generations through meaningful correspondence

Our Mission

Letters of Connection is dedicated to fostering intergenerational relationships through the timeless art of letter writing. We believe that connecting students with elderly community members creates meaningful bonds that enrich both lives, combat loneliness, and preserve valuable wisdom and stories for future generations.

Why Letter Writing?

Personal Connection

Handwritten letters create a deeper, more personal connection than digital communication.

Thoughtful Exchange

Letter writing encourages reflection and thoughtful communication between generations.

Lasting Impact

Letters become cherished keepsakes that can be revisited and treasured for years to come.
